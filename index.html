WORD FROM FOUNDER & CHAIRMAN

Being a cardiologist, alongside my partners who are also doctors and medical professionals, We take great pride in being the founders and partners of Taisier Healthcare Group (THG).The largest private tertiary healthcare operator in the Nile Delta region. a network of hospitals,labs and clinics dedicated to exceptional patient care. In 2006, driven by our shared belief that patient lives and comforts should be treated with the utmost care and the highest quality of service and products, we established Taisier-Med. Our vision was to provide patients everywhere with high-quality products to maintain their health with ease and convenience. While this ambition presented many challenges, our collective hard work and consistency led to the creation of our pioneering factory. This facility now manufactures some of the leading surgical sutures and wound closure devices in the market. We are pleased to have earned recognition as a leading business in the wound-closure sector in Egypt. We remain committed to ensuring that our superior products reach patients and customers both within Egypt and internationally.
