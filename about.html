About Company

Taisier-Med has been established in 2006 as a medical device production facility

providing a wide range of surgical sutures, mesh, and other wound closure devices. Our top-grade products, which are compliant to US and EU Pharmacopoeias, are supplied across local and global markets, meeting and surpassing quality and safety standards.

With Our Relentless Commitment To Innovation, Every Day Is A Whirlwind Of Fast-Paced Operations, Ensuring We Deliver Seamlessly And Swiftly. This is made possible by an expert team of labor, engineers, scientists, and technicians, guaranteeing utmost precision and reliability of all our products and services.

At our state-of-the-art facility, we blend our technology with expert craftsmanship to produce top-grade products that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

From Our Meticulous Processes To Our Unwavering Dedication To Healthcare Advancements, We're Shaping The Future Of Medical Solutions—One Suture At A Time.

Dr.Ahmed Gazar

CEO & co Founder

OVER THE YEARS, WE'VE DEDICATED OURSELVES TO RESEARCHING,APPLYING, AND EVOLVING OUR METHODS TO ENSURE THE RELIABILITY AND CONSISTENCY OF OUR THECHNOLOGY.

Quality Policy

We are contantly running through a quality system & a totality of management which ensures that products are consistently produced and controlled to the quality standards appropriate to their intended use and as required by the Marketing Authorization & product specification.

Customer Satisfaction

In this industry, customer relations are critical for success. Quick response times, personalized service, and consistent quality are key.


Research and development

In this manufacturing facility, Research and development (R&D) drives innovation and growth in our business. Our annual focus on developing new products ensures we stay competitive and meet evolving market demands. By investing in R&D, we enhance product quality, functionality, and efficiency, ultimately boosting sales and market share. Our commitment to innovation reinforces our position as a leader in the surgical sutures industry.

Mission and vision

By focusing on innovation, partnerships, and team satisfaction to elevate our life-saving products.
We aim to lead in key global markets by enhancing our design and manufacturing processes, prioritizing quality and exports.
Our goal is to be the top supplier globally, ensuring reliable access to our high-quality products worldwide.
