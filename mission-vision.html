  • Mission and Vision

We strive to be at the forefront of key global markets by continuously refining our design and manufacturing processes, with a sharp focus on quality and expanding our export reach.

Our ambition is to become the leading global supplier, guaranteeing dependable access to our high caliber products around the world.

To meet growing market demands swiftly, we are significantly increasing our production lines, ensuring that we can respond to needs with unmatched speed and efficiency.

Our Mission

  • We supply the Egyptian market with the best-in-class medical products, most patricularly wound-closure products.

  • We strive to become the number one supplier of such products globally, providing all patients with our products whenever they need it and wherever they are.

  • To get there, we're constantly working on anticipating the needs of our customers and the market by constantly refining our technology and production capabilities, while also establishing partnerships with public and private healthcare providers.

  • as well as investing heavily in the happiness and satisfaction of our teams of expert engineers, technicians, and administrative workers - all means to the end to taking the quality and distribution of our life-saving products to the next level.

Our Vision

  • Driven by our ambition to be a trusted and reliable partner to healthcare providers , we aim to continue being at the helm of the wound-closure industry.

  • We aspire and work towards being one of the leaders of such crucial products in the global market aiming to reach this goal by focused dedication to upgrade our design and manufacturing processes in order to continue building up the most reliable brand.

  • Furthermore, we are working towards increasing the export capabilities to serve markets across the region and the world.

  • Through a focus on quality and optimizing processes, we aim to become an export powerhouse in the medium and long term.
